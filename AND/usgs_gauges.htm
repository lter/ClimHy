USGS Data Harvesting Service for ClimDB/HydroDB

Attention to all sites that have USGS maintained gauges! With very little effort on your part, we can now harvest data directly from the real-time USGS web site with their full approval. Thanks primarily to the efforts of Wade Sheldon (Georgia Coastal Ecosystem LTER), as well as the ClimDB/HydroDB Development Team, real-time data can now be accessed from the real-time USGS web site and automatically harvested into ClimDB/HydroDB.
